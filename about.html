About Me

Hi, I'm Barbara Gilmore, a software developer and web and applications designer based in San Antonio, Texas. I have a passion for creating beautiful and functional web apps that solve real-world problems. I enjoy learning new technologies and tools, and collaborating with other developers and designers.

Some of the technologies and tools that I use or want to learn are:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Node.js
  • React
  • Jest
  • Bootstrap
  • Git
  • GitHub
  • VS Code

I have always had an interest in what's behind the scenes and the digital blueprint and creation of the internet, as well as applications and video games. I joined the Navy right out of high school and went straight to work as an Avionics Installer and Electrical Engineer for helicopters in the Navy, all while living in Japan. I fulfilled my contract and then went overseas as a contractor for the Army, doing the same. After that contract, I pursued my associate's aviation degrees and completed my programs for both Airframe and Powerplant sciences. I am deciding a big change in my career, not only because I want to do something even more challenging, but because I also want to be a creator and continue to be a role model for my girls; so that they know they can do anything. When I'm not coding, I like to read books, write poetry, watch movies, play video games, and travel to new places.

If you want to know more about me, feel free to contact me at barbara.gilmore@fullstacksolutions.com.
